Why A First Principle Approach?

First Principles Thinking is a critical thinking process of boiling things down to help you understand how things work.

It helps you identify what matters. Think for a moment of what you believe is the purpose of financial planning?

It helps you identify what matters. Think for a moment of what you believe is the purpose of financial planning? If you boil things down, what is at the core of planning? Once you start considering your options for different strategies, we often face information overload

We Use a First Principle Approach due to POLARIZATION

Polarization makes it very hard to know what to believe and what's the best path for you in your retirement. Polarizing beliefs are often associated with topics like:

POLITICS

This is currently playing out in our everyday life today.

RELIGION

Religion is another highly polarizing topic that many of us experience, making it tough to know what is true.

MONEY

Money is the most polarizing subject. If you went to ten different financial advisors, how many different opinions would you hear?

Then, if you start researching how to invest for retirement online, the polarizing views get even more extreme.
